Agentic AI · Tool-Calling SystemShipped

AI Support Triage Agent

An agent that routes support tickets across three actions — draft, escalate, or clarify — using native function-calling, not freeform text parsing. Hard-coded policy rules override model judgment for security/legal tickets, regardless of how confident the model is. Built a labeled eval harness that measures routing accuracy separately from hard-trigger escalation recall, because those two numbers mean very different things.
