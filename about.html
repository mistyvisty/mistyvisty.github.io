About

Trained in engineering, working in responsible AI.

I'm a Data Analyst and ML Engineer — trained as an Electronics & Communication engineer with a strong foundation in maths, statistics, and systems thinking.

My work lives at the intersection of rigorous ML engineering and real business impact. I don't just build models that score well — I ask whether the features make business sense, whether the pipeline has data leakage, and whether the output actually drives a decision.

I've now moved into Generative AI — building RAG pipelines grounded in real documents, with hallucination-aware prompting baked in from the start. My Medical RAG Assistant answers clinical questions about PCOS from actual research papers, citing the source page for every answer. My Support Triage Agent hard-codes escalation rules for security and legal tickets, so the highest-stakes decisions never depend on a model's confidence alone.

I also write technical articles explaining ML and GenAI concepts from first principles — because understanding why a technique works matters more than knowing that it works.
